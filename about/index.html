About

Involved with computers since the early age of 5, back when personal computers were not mainstream, Raúl became passionate about computing technologies, programming languages and videogames. He has been using and enjoying computers since the DOS/pre-Windows era.

Raúl is mostly experienced in C/C++, .NET (C#, ASP.NET MVC, .NET Core), JavaScript/HTML5 and computer building/repairing, but he can easily adapt/quickly learn the tools, frameworks or programming languages needed for the job at hand. He has been recetly interested in Flutter and Information Security. He is self-taught and enjoys learning new things. In his free time, he enjoys coding for fun and gaming on powerful PC hardware. He also enjoys playing the piano and exercising.

Aside from Spanish, which is his native language, he also speaks English. Il parle aussi le français. He actually learns languages by playing text-heavy videogames, as freaky as that may sound.
