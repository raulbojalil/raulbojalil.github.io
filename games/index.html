Cheese Shooter

Ready for some cheese shooting action? Shoot bouncy pieces of cheese at the hungry mouse but be careful since you have only limited amounts of cheese and the cheese pieces break easily. Avoid obstacles and enemies as you try to collect coins using the minimum number of bounces possible.

FEATURES:

  • Easy to play
  • Shoot your way through 100 levels in 4 different environments (grasslands, city, desert and space)
  • Tricky puzzles and obstacles
  • Suitable for the whole family
